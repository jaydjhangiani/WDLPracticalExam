The purpose of the report is to analyse the factors influencing food choices and purchases.In this survey we broadly classify the decisions and actions that influence purchase behaviour of the customer.A questionnaire was prepared, circulated and collected from people living in different regions in Mumbai. Survey will provide detailed information about factors which influence a person to buy a certain food product.Surveys like these do not only help us understand the choices made in current scenario but also predict future trends.In order to get a clear understanding we emphasize on the factors like Cost, Quality,Quantity,Advertisement,Convenience and Brand.
